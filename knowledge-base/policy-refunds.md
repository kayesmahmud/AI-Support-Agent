# Refund and Returns Policy

## Refund Eligibility
- Products can be returned within 30 days of purchase
- Item must be unused and in original packaging
- Digital products are non-refundable after download

## How to Request a Refund
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Select the order and click "Request Refund"
4. Our team will review within 2-3 business days

## Refund Processing Time
- Credit card refunds: 5-10 business days
- PayPal refunds: 3-5 business days
- Store credit: Instant

## Exceptions
- Sale items are final sale
- Personalized/custom items cannot be returned
- Subscription refunds are prorated

## Contact for Refund Issues
If your refund is delayed or you have questions, contact billing@company.com with your order number.
